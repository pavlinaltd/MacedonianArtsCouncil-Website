Young Brides, Old Treasures monograph was published jointly by the Macedonian Arts Council and the Museum of International Folk Art in Santa Fe, New Mexico. This truly beautiful study documents Macedonian folk art through five articles on topics ranging from embroidery and weaving techniques to the social significance of the costumesâ€™ creation. The book contains 170 pages of full-scale photographs of the permanent collection of Macedonian bridal costumes donated to the Museum, making it the second largest collection in the western hemisphere. On display at MOIFA from December 2011 through January 6, 2013, these unique folk artifacts testify to the creative genius of the Macedonian embroideresses, keepers of Macedonian cultural identity.
